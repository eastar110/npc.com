Short Bio

Zhang Dong, Ph.D., associate professor, doctoral supervisor, is currently the dean of the computer science department of Fuzhou University and the dean of the computer engineering department of Fuzhou University Zhicheng College. Undergraduate, master, and doctoral studies at Zhejiang University. In 2010, he graduated with a PhD in Computer Science and Technology from Zhejiang University. As a state-sponsored visiting scholar, he went to the University of Alabama in the United States from September 2018 to September 2019. Mainly engaged in software-defined networking, medical artificial intelligence, etc., he has published dozens of papers in well-known conferences or journals at home and abroad such as INFOCOM, ICNP, GLOBECOM, IM, JPDC, and Journal of Electronics.
